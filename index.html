MAGE Con 2022 -- July 22-23, 2022


Welcome Gamers!

MAGE Con 2022 has been set for July 22-23, 2022. It will be held at the Mahomet American Legion, 412 East Main Street, Mahomet, IL

The MAGES (Mahomet Area Gaming EnthusiastS) invite you to join us as we get back to in-person gaming!
For primary game information and signups, see our MAGE Con 2022 Warhorn page.

Giveaways:

We are excited about giving away two items this year:
1) As usual, all attendees will get a MAGE Con patch (designed by Greg Ziegler and Mark Webb).


2) For the first 50 registrants who ALSO attend/participate (must be present), we will also be giving out a limited edition, commemorative MAGE Con miniature, sculpted and cast by local gamers Keegan and Keith Pogue. This mini was modeled after our first MAGE Con mage (as seen in our 2018 patch).

For general questions, feel free to email:  archmage@magecon.org
