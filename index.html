MAGE Con 8 -- June 26 & 27, 2026


Welcome Gamers!

MAGE Con 8 has been set for June 26 & 27, 2026 (Friday and Saturday).
It will be at the same two locations as last year -- the Mahomet American Legion and the Mahomet Masonic Lodge. (American Legion = 412 East Main Street; Masonic Lodge = 414 East Main Street).

The MAGES (Mahomet Area Gaming EnthusiastS) invite you to join us as we continue our gaming tradition into its 8th year! It's hard to believe it's been 8 years already, but... here we are.
For primary game information and signups, see our MAGE Con 2026 Warhorn page. It is empty right now, but have another look as we add content in early 2026.
