MAGE Con 2025 -- June 27-28, 2025


Welcome Gamers!

MAGE Con 2025 has been set for June 27 & 28, 2025 (Friday and Saturday).
It will be at the same general location this year, but we are expanding to include both the Mahomet American Legion and the Mahomet Masonic Lodge. (American Legion = 412 East Main Street; Masonic Lodge = 414 East Main Street).

The MAGES (Mahomet Area Gaming EnthusiastS) invite you to join us as we continue our gaming tradition into its 7th year! Thanks go out to all previous participants! We hope you can make it again!
For primary game information and signups, see our MAGE Con 2025 Warhorn page.

We have added many games to the schedule but will be adding a few more, likely through the end of April. Keep checking back for the most complete schedule. Games so far include:

  • Dungeons & Dragons (both homebrew and Adventurers League)
  • Pathfinder/Starfinder Organized Play
  • Dune Uprising (with Expansions) Board Game
  • Miniature battles, including 60mm Space Hulk, and Mythical Rome Gladiators
  • HeroQuest
  • Call of Cthulhu
  • Imperial RPG

Stay tuned!
