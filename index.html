MAGE Con 2024 -- July 12-13, 2024


Welcome Gamers!

MAGE Con 2024 has been set for July 12 & 13, 2024 (Friday and Saturday). It will be held at the Mahomet American Legion, 412 East Main Street, Mahomet, IL

The MAGES (Mahomet Area Gaming EnthusiastS) invite you to join us as we continue our gaming tradition into its 6th year! Thanks go out to all previous participants! We hope you can make it again!
For primary game information and signups, see our MAGE Con 2024 Warhorn page.
