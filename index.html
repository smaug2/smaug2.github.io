MAGE Con 2019

Jul 12-13 in Mahomet, IL, US


Welcome Gamers!

The MAGES (Mahomet Area Gaming Enthusiasts) invite you to join us for our second annual MAGE Con!  We have expanded this year to 2 days , Friday and Saturday, July 12th and 13th, 2019. We hope you will help us keep the tradition going. 

Please be aware that Warhorn has a timezone setting (set for each user) that may cause confusion in starting times.  Our events will be running 8 AM until 10 PM Central Time Zone.

Location:

Mahomet Community Center, 510 E. Main Street, Mahomet, IL 61853

The event will feature Dungeons and Dragons 5e Adventurer's League play, but we may be offering some other choices. Details will be posted closer to the event date.
