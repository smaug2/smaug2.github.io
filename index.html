MAGE Con 2019

July 12-13 in Mahomet, IL, US


Welcome Gamers!

The MAGES (Mahomet Area Gaming EnthusiastS) invite you to join us for our second annual MAGE Con!  We have expanded this year to 2 days , Friday and Saturday, July 12th and 13th, 2019. We hope you will help us keep the tradition going. 

While Dungeons and Dragons 5e Adventurer's League play wlll be a large part of MAGE Con, this year we are excited to welcome our friends from the local Pathfinder & Starfinder Society Organized Play group. We hope that no matter which game system you prefer, you'll have a great time at this year's convention.

Once we open registration, signups will be done through Warhorn: MAGE Con 2019
